6-Month Study Plan for CDS Mathematics

This plan covers all topics from basics to advanced levels and includes weekly goals to keep you on track. Adjust the pace as needed based on your progress and comfort level with each topic.

Month 1: Foundation Building

Week 1: Number System & Fundamental Operations

  • Study: Natural numbers, Integers, Rational numbers, Real numbers.
  • Practice: Basic operations (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division) with whole numbers and fractions.
  • Review: Divisibility rules and HCF/LCM.
  • Resources: Textbooks, online tutorials.

Week 2: Decimal Fractions & Percentages

  • Study: Conversion between fractions and decimals, operations with decimal numbers.
  • Practice: Percentage calculations, profit & loss, discount problems.
  • Review: Important percentage formulas.
  • Resources: Practice problems from textbooks or online platforms.

Week 3: Ratio, Proportion & Time, Work, Distance

  • Study: Ratio and proportion concepts, applications.
  • Practice: Problems involving time, work, distance, speed.
  • Review: Key formulas and typical problem-solving approaches.
  • Resources: Practice worksheets and online quizzes.

Week 4: Simple and Compound Interest

  • Study: Basic concepts of simple and compound interest.
  • Practice: Problems on calculating interest and amount.
  • Review: Formulas for simple and compound interest.
  • Resources: Study guides, previous year papers.

Month 2: Intermediate Concepts

Week 5: Elementary Number Theory

  • Study: Division algorithm, prime and composite numbers.
  • Practice: Factorization theorem, multiples and factors.
  • Review: Logarithms basics and their applications.
  • Resources: Number theory exercises, tutorials.

Week 6: Algebra - Basics

  • Study: Algebraic expressions, basic operations.
  • Practice: Simplification and basic factorization.
  • Review: Important algebraic identities.
  • Resources: Algebra textbooks, online problem sets.

Week 7: Algebra - Advanced Topics

  • Study: HCF and LCM of polynomials, theory of polynomials.
  • Practice: Remainder theorem, quadratic equations.
  • Review: Solving simultaneous linear equations.
  • Resources: Advanced algebra practice problems.

Week 8: Algebra - Further Practice

  • Study: Set theory, rational expressions, laws of indices.
  • Practice: Comprehensive algebraic problems.
  • Review: Key concepts and problem-solving techniques.
  • Resources: Algebra problem books, online quizzes.

Month 3: Trigonometry

Week 9: Trigonometric Ratios

  • Study: Sine, cosine, tangent functions.
  • Practice: Basic problems involving trigonometric ratios.
  • Review: Values of trigonometric functions for standard angles.
  • Resources: Trigonometry textbooks, online resources.

Week 10: Trigonometric Identities

  • Study: Fundamental trigonometric identities.
  • Practice: Applying identities to solve problems.
  • Review: Trigonometric tables usage.
  • Resources: Practice exercises and online tutorials.

Week 11: Heights and Distances

  • Study: Problems related to heights and distances using trigonometry.
  • Practice: Application of trigonometric ratios in practical problems.
  • Review: Important problem-solving strategies.
  • Resources: Trigonometry problem sets.

Week 12: Comprehensive Trigonometry Review

  • Study: Advanced trigonometric problems.
  • Practice: Mixed problem sets covering all trigonometric concepts.
  • Review: Key concepts and formulas.
  • Resources: Practice papers and mock tests.

Month 4: Geometry

Week 13: Lines and Angles

  • Study: Properties of lines, angles, and their relationships.
  • Practice: Problems on lines and angles.
  • Review: Key theorems and properties.
  • Resources: Geometry textbooks, online exercises.

Week 14: Triangles

  • Study: Properties of different types of triangles, theorems related to triangles.
  • Practice: Problems on congruence and similarity.
  • Review: Key theorems on triangles.
  • Resources: Practice worksheets, geometry guides.

Week 15: Circles

  • Study: Properties of circles, tangents, chords, arcs.
  • Practice: Problems involving circles.
  • Review: Key properties and theorems.
  • Resources: Circle problems from textbooks.

Week 16: Parallelograms, Rectangles, Squares & Loci

  • Study: Properties of parallelograms, rectangles, squares, and the concept of loci.
  • Practice: Problems involving these shapes.
  • Review: Key properties and theorems.
  • Resources: Geometry problem sets.

Month 5: Mensuration & Statistics

Week 17: Mensuration Basics

  • Study: Calculation of areas of plane figures (squares, rectangles, triangles, circles).
  • Practice: Problems on area calculation.
  • Review: Formulas and problem-solving techniques.
  • Resources: Mensuration exercises.

Week 18: Surface Areas and Volumes

  • Study: Surface areas and volumes of 3D figures (cuboids, cylinders, cones, spheres).
  • Practice: Problems involving surface areas and volumes.
  • Review: Important formulas and applications.
  • Resources: Mensuration problem books.

Week 19: Statistics - Basics

  • Study: Collection and tabulation of data.
  • Practice: Problems on frequency tables.
  • Review: Key concepts in data collection and organization.
  • Resources: Statistics textbooks, online resources.

Week 20: Statistics - Advanced Topics

  • Study: Graphical representation (bar graphs, histograms, pie charts).
  • Practice: Problems on drawing and interpreting graphs.
  • Review: Measures of central tendency (mean, median, mode).
  • Resources: Statistics practice papers.

Month 6: Revision & Mock Tests

Week 21: Revision of Arithmetic and Number Theory

  • Review: Key concepts and formulas in arithmetic and number theory.
  • Practice: Mixed problem sets covering all topics.
  • Resources: Revision notes, previous year papers.

Week 22: Revision of Algebra and Trigonometry

  • Review: Advanced concepts in algebra and trigonometry.
  • Practice: Comprehensive problem sets.
  • Resources: Revision guides, mock tests.

Week 23: Revision of Geometry and Mensuration

  • Review: Key properties and formulas in geometry and mensuration.
  • Practice: Mixed problem sets.
  • Resources: Geometry and mensuration notes.

Week 24: Full-Length Mock Tests & Final Revision

  • Practice: Full-length mock tests under exam conditions.
  • Review: Analyze results, focus on weak areas.
  • Resources: Mock test papers, detailed solutions.

Additional Tips

  • Daily Study Time: Allocate 2-3 hours daily for studying mathematics.
  • Regular Breaks: Take short breaks to avoid burnout.
  • Consistency: Follow the plan consistently and adjust based on progress.
  • Feedback: Regularly assess your performance through quizzes and mock tests.
